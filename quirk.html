About the Project

The goal of this project was to create an interactive prototype focused on the user experience and UI/UX design for an App based on the creator's vision.

I worked with a group of 3 other Brown University students to design a prototype for Quirk, an app that provides a platform for journaling, personal reflection, accountability, and improvement with regards to mental health. According to the startup's website, Quirk implements the popular cognitive behavioral therapy exercise of catching, checking, and changing the problem at hand. This kind of thinking can help people combat a variety of mental health concerns. â€‹
